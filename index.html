Hello, I'm Ping!


A PhD Candidate in Innovation, Technology & Operations at Rady School, UC San Diego.

My research examines how incentives stimulate business innovation and improve overall outcomes on data science related platforms. My primary expertise is in game theory, machine learning, GenAI technology and its applications to the context of product and service innovation.
